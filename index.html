Forbidden Rite: Ward, Life, and Chaos Resistance

If your Forbidden Rite damage is greater than your Ward, then don't use Mind Over Matter, because Forbidden Rite damage will remove your mana and also you cannot run less recovery map mods. It does not break the build, but you have to be careful.

If you have more life, you need to adjust chaos resistance to avoid FR damage to life pool. This applies only if you are using Forbidden Rite trigger setup.

Cast when Damage Taken gem levels and quality

If any CwDT gem has a higher effective level or lower quality than the gem in your body armour, repeat the calculation for both gems.

The CwDT gem linked to Forbidden Rite does not take damage from Forbidden Rite (it is on cooldown when the damage happens). It must be low enough to be triggered by Skeleton damage alone.

About Flasks

Try to get flask coefficent above 1.03. Anything above 1.0 should theoritically work, but does not sometimes!
You can find your flasks stats in your character sheet. Press C and go to Misc Tab.
